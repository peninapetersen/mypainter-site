Meet Richo

Painter, handyman, Whangarei local — 25+ years on the tools.

Gidday — I'm Richard, but everyone calls me Richo.

I've been painting and fixing things in Northland homes for over 25 years. I started young, learned the trade hands-on, and built my reputation one happy customer at a time.

No gimmicks. No fine print. No surprises on the invoice.

Just honest work, fair prices, and a clean site when I leave.

I'm based in Glenbervie and I cover all of Whangarei and the surrounding 50km for no travel fee. For jobs further out in Northland — Kerikeri, Mangawhai, Dargaville, Kaikohe — I'm still happy to come, just with a small travel fee worked into the quote.

Whether it's a full exterior repaint, a single room refresh, a roof that's seen better days, or that list of odd jobs you've been putting off — give me a ring. I'll come have a look, give you a fair quote, and if you want me to do it, I'll do it properly.
